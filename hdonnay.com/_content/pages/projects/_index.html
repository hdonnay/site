--- title: Projects --- This is a page about my projects that I'm sharing with the internet at large. ####A word on licenses: I'm not a huge fan of the GNU General Public License, and try to avoid it when working on my own code. The main reason I dislike it is that it infects any project that wants to copy it. I feel this is kind of against the spirit of Free Software. For the software I write and release that I feel needs a license, I use a modified X11/MIT license that disallows commercial use.
